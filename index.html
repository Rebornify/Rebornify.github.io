The Beginning

Posted on 29th April 2020

My name is Caleb, and I am currently serving the army in Singapore. This project will be a great opportunity to log my progress so far as well as in the future. Mathematics have always been my favourite subject in school, but my passion for gaming is just as intense. I was always intrigued by bugs that occur in games, wondering why would it happen. After completing my A Levels, I was met with a big decision in my education pathway, what am I going to study in University, Mathematics? What am I going to do afterwards? Eventually, I managed to solve the dilemma after coming across Computer Science, encompassing both my passion for Mathematics as well as computing. While serving the country, I wanted to pick up programming so I would be better prepared for University, and hence me writing this blog here.
